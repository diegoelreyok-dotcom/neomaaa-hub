**Mode:** Remote (must align with LATAM hours: 9:00–18:00 COL/MEX)

**Start:** Immediate

---

## Why this position is PRIORITY #1

The Finance Manager is the most critical pending hire at NEOMAAA Markets. Without this person, the broker cannot:

- Accurately track the flow of funds between PSPs, trading accounts and corporate bank accounts.
- Process withdrawals within regulatory and competitive timeframes.
- Generate reliable financial reports for the Principals.
- Detect reconciliation discrepancies before they become losses.
- Maintain healthy relationships with payment providers (PSPs).

Without a dedicated Finance Manager, the Principals absorb these functions and divert their attention from growing the business. This role pays for itself by avoiding reconciliation errors and optimizing payment processing costs.

---

## Role Overview

The Finance Manager is responsible for full financial management of the broker: deposit and withdrawal processing, relationships with payment service providers (PSPs), daily reconciliation, P&L tracking, cash flow management, and financial reporting to the Principals. They operate as the central hub for all of the company's financial information.

---

## Responsibilities

### Deposits and Withdrawals
1. Supervise and process all client deposit and withdrawal requests via Skale CRM and PSP panels.
2. Verify each transaction meets compliance requirements before approving withdrawals.
3. Escalate withdrawals that exceed defined thresholds or show irregularities to the Compliance team and Principals.

### PSP Relationships
4. Manage relationships with all payment service providers (currently 120+ deposit methods available).
5. Negotiate fees, resolve transaction disputes, and ensure continuous availability of key payment methods for each target market (LATAM, Middle East, Africa, SE Asia).
6. Evaluate and recommend new PSPs based on market needs and operating costs.

### Reconciliation
7. Execute daily reconciliation between Skale CRM records, PSP panels, and corporate bank accounts.
8. Identify and resolve discrepancies immediately, documenting each case and its resolution.

### Financial Reports and P&L
9. Prepare weekly and monthly financial reports for the Principals including: P&L, cash flow, deposit/withdrawal volume, PSP costs, and key metrics.
10. Maintain financial dashboards updated with real-time data or no more than 24 hours of lag.

### Cash Flow and Planning
11. Manage company cash flow ensuring sufficient liquidity for withdrawals, payroll, operating costs and regulatory reserves.
12. Alert the Principals in advance when liquidity bottlenecks are projected.

### Payroll and Operating Costs
13. Process monthly team payroll (~$21K current, projected ~$34K at full Stage 1) per the established calendar.
14. Track and categorize all operating expenses: software licenses, server costs, marketing, external vendors.

### Financial Compliance
15. Ensure all financial operations comply with regulatory requirements of the AOFA license.
16. Collaborate with the Compliance Officer on suspicious transaction reports (STR) and record keeping.

### Optimization
17. Identify opportunities to reduce payment processing costs and improve execution times.
18. Propose and execute financial process improvements as the broker scales.
