# Broker roles — Full organizational structure

**Company:** Neomaaa Ltd (IBC 15968) | License L15968/N (AOFA, Anjouan)
**Use:** Internal reference for hiring, onboarding, KPI definition and organizational design
**Audience:** Founders, department Heads, HR, new employees
**Last updated:** April 2026

---

## Introduction

### Why this document exists

NEOMAAA Markets is in pre-launch. Each month that passes, the organization grows: 16 people today, in 12 months we'll be 35-50, and in 24 months potentially 80+. Without a clear map of roles, growth becomes chaos: we hire what's urgent instead of what's important, employees don't know what's expected of them, Heads overlap, and cashflow misaligns with real priorities.

This document solves four concrete problems:

1. **Hiring decisions with criteria.** Know which role to hire now, which in 3 months, and which can wait 12 months depending on broker stage.
2. **Clear KPIs per role.** Every employee must know what's measured in their job and how "good" vs "bad" is scored.
3. **Expectation setting.** Each role has a real function, reports it produces, and dependencies with other roles. No one can say "I didn't know".
4. **Systemic view of the broker.** Anyone on the team should be able to open this doc and understand where their role fits in the full puzzle.

> [!INFO]
> This is not an individual job description. It is the full operational map. Specific JDs (like [Finance Manager](/en/hiring/finance-manager) or [Support Agent EN](/en/hiring/support-en)) expand each role with operational detail.

### How a broker is organized

A regulated forex/CFD broker is structured in three vertical layers plus a fourth horizontal layer of external services:

<div className="neo-pyramid">
<div className="neo-pyramid-level">Layer 1 — Governance / C-Level (4-6 people)</div>
<div className="neo-pyramid-level">Layer 2 — Department Heads (5-8 people)</div>
<div className="neo-pyramid-level">Layer 3 — Operational roles (60-75% of headcount)</div>
<div className="neo-pyramid-level">Layer 4 — External / outsourceable roles (retainer or project)</div>
</div>

<div className="neo-step-list">

**Layer 1 — Governance / C-Level.** They decide strategy, allocate capital, answer to the regulator and the board. 4-6 people at a mature broker. The only roles that cannot be outsourced.

**Layer 2 — Department Heads.** Turn strategy into execution. Each Head owns a department (Sales, Support, Marketing, Dealing, Finance). They have quarterly targets and their own teams.

**Layer 3 — Operational roles.** The daily execution force: sales agents close FTDs, support agents resolve tickets, dealing operators monitor execution, devs maintain the stack, marketing specialists run campaigns. 60-75% of total headcount lives here.

**Layer 4 — External / outsourceable roles.** Legal counsel, external auditor, multi-jurisdiction accountants, security consultants. Hired on retainer or project, not full-time.

</div>

| Stage | Headcount | Must-have | Nice-to-have | Avoid |
|-------|-----------|-----------|--------------|--------|
| **Pre-launch + 6 months** | 10-15 | CEO, COO, Compliance, Dealing, Finance Manager, Head of Sales, 3-4 Sales agents, 2 Support agents | CFO, Head of Marketing, CSM | CTO (senior devs are enough), HR Manager |

> Most common mistake: hiring a CTO or Head of Marketing too early. Until there are 2-3 engineers or 2-3 marketing specialists to manage, the role has no one to lead and becomes a senior doer with manager pay.

# LAYER 1 — GOVERNANCE / C-LEVEL

Strategic decision roles. Report to the board or equity partners. Not outsourced.

## CEO (Chief Executive Officer)

**Real function.** Defines broker vision and strategy. Allocates capital between departments (marketing vs tech vs compliance). Closes strategic deals with PSPs, liquidity providers, and institutional partners. The public face with regulators, press, investors. Makes decisions no one else can: pivots, fundraising, layoffs, jurisdiction changes. Protects culture and vision as the team grows.

**Reports generated.**
- **Monthly board pack.** Consolidated P&L, cashflow, OKR progress, top 5 risks, pending decisions.
- **Quarterly report to equity partners.** Revenue, EBITDA, milestones achieved, capital usage, 12-month roadmap.
- **Investor update (if any).** Monthly or quarterly per agreement.
- **Internal team communication.** Monthly all-hands with numbers, priorities, organizational changes.

**Reports received.**
- Daily executive dashboard (deposits, withdrawals, active traders, cashflow).
- Weekly report from each department Head.
- Weekly P&L from Finance.
- Compliance alerts on critical issues.
- Incident reports from Tech and Dealing.

**KPIs measured.**

| KPI | Target | Frequency |
|-----|----------|------------|
| Total monthly revenue | 15-25% MoM growth in stage 1 | Monthly |
| EBITDA margin | >25% in stage 2, >35% in stage 3 | Monthly |
| FTDs/month | Quarterly plan agreed with Head of Sales | Weekly |
| 90-day client retention | >40% | Monthly |
| Runway in months | Minimum 12, ideal 18+ | Weekly |
| Company NPS | >40 (clients) and >50 (employees) | Quarterly |
| Regulator findings | 0 major, 0-2 minor/year | Annual |

**Ideal profile.** Founder or ex-CEO/COO in fintech, prop firm or broker. Mix of business + product + sales. Experience closing deals with PSPs, LPs or regulators. Ideally multilingual (Spanish + English minimum). High tolerance for operational and financial risk. 10+ years of executive career or proven founder.

**Reports to.** Board / equity partners / principals.

**Manages.** All department Heads (COO, CFO, CCO, CTO, Head of Sales, Head of Marketing, Head of Dealing, Head of Support).

**Critical dependencies.** Compliance (total legal exposure), Finance (daily cashflow), Sales (revenue), Dealing (broker risk).

**Market cost.** $8K-25K/month (LATAM), $15K-50K/month (EU/UAE). In early-stage frequently equity-paid with low base salary.

**At NEOMAAA.** Diego holds this role as founder/CEO. No replacement or backup plan in the next 24 months.

**Risk if vacant.** The company loses direction in 30 days. Strategic decisions stall, Heads fight over priorities, investors lose trust, the team fragments. A broker cannot exist without a CEO.

## COO (Chief Operating Officer)

**Real function.** Operationalizes the CEO's strategy. Ensures every department executes the quarterly OKRs. Owner of cross-functional processes: client onboarding, withdrawal flow, escalations between Sales/Support/Compliance, operational quality. Resolves conflicts between Heads. Handles the "human" side of the business — culture, performance reviews, hiring senior roles. Frees CEO time from operational topics so the CEO can focus on growth and strategy.

**Reports generated.**
- **Operations weekly review.** Department SLAs, bottlenecks, actions for the week.
- **Monthly ops review to CEO.** Headcount, turnover, training gaps, broken processes, initiative ROI.
- **Escalation report.** Critical cases resolved and lessons learned.

**KPIs measured.**
- Department SLAs met >95% (Support FRT, Finance reconciliation, Compliance KYC, Dealing uptime).
- Client NPS >40.
- Employee turnover <15%/year.
- Operating costs as % of revenue <40%.
- On-time delivery of quarterly initiatives >80%.

**Ideal profile.** Operator with 8+ years in fintech/broker/prop firm. Strong in processes (Lean, Six Sigma plus), people management, and operational numbers. Doesn't need to be technical — needs to make the machine work. Ability to say "no" and prioritize.

**Reports to.** CEO.

**Manages.** Heads of Support, Marketing, Sales, Dealing (indirectly), plus HR Manager and Office Managers.

**Critical dependencies.** CEO (strategy), department Heads (execution), CFO (operating budget).

**Market cost.** $7K-20K/month (LATAM), $12K-30K/month (EU/UAE).

**At NEOMAAA.** Yulia covers part of this function today combined with her Principal role. The role will be formalized when headcount exceeds 30 people.

**When to hire formally.** Stage 2 (6-18 months post-launch), with 25-30 employees. Before then, the CEO can assume the function without losing focus.

**Risk if vacant.** Departments operate in silos. Cross-functional processes break (a Sales-closed lead never reaches Compliance cleanly). The CEO drowns in operations and doesn't do strategy.

## CFO (Chief Financial Officer)

**Real function.** Total financial health of the company. Operational and strategic cashflow. Annual budget and reforecasts. Consolidated monthly P&L. Fundraising (if applicable). Relations with banks and auditors. Taxes and multi-jurisdiction fiscal planning (critical for an offshore + onshore broker). Valuation for rounds or M&A. Long-term financial models.

**Reports generated.**
- **Monthly consolidated P&L.** Revenue by source, costs by category, EBITDA, net margin. Closed <5 business days post-month.
- **Weekly cashflow.** Cash position in each account (corporate, operating, PSP holding, regulatory), 90-day projection.
- **Monthly budget vs actual.** Variances per department, explanations, corrective actions.
- **Quarterly financial board pack.** P&L, BS, CF, key metrics, benchmarks.
- **Tax filings.** Per each jurisdiction's calendar (Anjouan, additional corporate structure, etc.).

**KPIs measured.**

| KPI | Target |
|-----|----------|
| Cash position | Always >3 months of OpEx |
| Runway | >12 months stage 1, >18 stage 2 |
| EBITDA margin | >25% stage 2, >35% stage 3 |
| Gross margin | >60% |
| Client CAC | <30% of LTV |
| Client LTV | >3x CAC |
| Monthly days-to-close | <5 business days |
| Reconciliation accuracy | 100% |

**Ideal profile.** CPA or CFA. Experience at a broker, regulated fintech, or scale-up. Comfort with multi-currency (USD, EUR, LATAM local currencies), multi-jurisdiction, multi-PSP. Strong in Excel/Google Sheets and consolidation tools (NetSuite, advanced Xero). Fluent English. Ideally Big Four audit background.

**Reports to.** CEO.

**Manages.** Finance Manager, Finance Junior, external Accountants.

**Critical dependencies.** CEO (capital strategy), Compliance (regulatory capital), COO (operating budget), Finance Manager (daily data).

**Market cost.** $8K-20K/month (LATAM), $15K-35K/month (EU/UAE). Fractional CFO: $3K-7K/month part-time.

**At NEOMAAA.** Vacant. Critical to hire. For now Diego + Yulia cover partially. Temporary alternative: fractional CFO while full-time hire closes.

**When to hire.** Late stage 1 or start of stage 2 (3-6 months post-launch). More risk if it doesn't arrive before the first 12 months of active operation.

**Risk if vacant.** Blind financial decisions. Tax compliance at risk (fines for late or bad filings). Cashflow becomes reactive. Fundraising impossible without auditable financial structure.

## CTO (Chief Technology Officer)

**Real function.** Full broker tech stack. Infrastructure (servers, redundancy, disaster recovery). Security (pen testing, encryption, access control). Development team. Owner of MT5 setup (admin, bridges, plugins), critical integrations (Skale CRM, Sumsub KYC, PSPs, Intercom), client portal, mobile apps. Digital product roadmap. Build vs buy decisions.

**Reports generated.**
- **Weekly tech health.** Uptime of each system, weekly incidents, deploys, bug queue.
- **Incident report.** Each major incident (downtime, breach, data loss) with RCA and actions.
- **Monthly security report.** Vulnerabilities detected and resolved, pen test status, IT compliance (SOC2 if applicable).
- **Quarterly roadmap.** Priority features, timelines, resources needed.

**KPIs measured.**
- Client portal and MT5 uptime >99.9%.
- Incident count <2 major/month.
- Median time-to-recover <30 minutes.
- Deploy frequency: at least 1 production deploy/week.
- Security score (external scan) >90/100.
- Critical pen test findings closed in <30 days.

**Ideal profile.** Senior dev or architect with 10+ years. Fintech/trading experience required. Knows MT5 admin, FIX protocol, cloud infra (AWS/GCP), Node/Python/TS, SQL+NoSQL databases, security (OWASP top 10, encryption, IAM). Fluent English. Ability to manage distributed remote teams.

**Reports to.** CEO.

**Manages.** Senior devs (backend, frontend, mobile), DevOps, IT Security, QA.

**Critical dependencies.** Head of Dealing (execution tech), Compliance (data retention, audit trails), CFO (tech budget).

**Market cost.** $6K-18K/month (LATAM), $12K-30K/month (EU/UAE).

**At NEOMAAA.** Roles distributed among current devs (Alex A, Alex B, Gleb, Dimitri). No formal CTO yet. The role is justified once there are 5+ engineers and a roadmap to coordinate.

**When to hire.** Stage 3 (18+ months). Before that, a senior Tech Lead or fractional CTO covers the role at significantly lower cost.

**Risk if vacant.** Uncoordinated tech decisions. Tech debt grows without a payment plan. Security gaps without an owner. Slow incident response.

## CCO (Chief Compliance Officer)

**Real function.** Ensures the broker complies with AOFA regulation (Anjouan Offshore Finance Authority), FATF AML/CFT guidelines, international sanctions (OFAC, UN, EU), and local rules of each market where it operates (Spain CNMV, Brazil CVM as competitive reference, etc.). Decides client KYC approval/rejection. Reports suspicious activities (SAR) to the regulator. Owner of the full AML program. Audits suspicious deposits/withdrawals. Protects the broker from sanctions, fines, and loss of license.

**Reports generated.**
- **Monthly compliance to the board.** KYC stats, AML alerts, sanctions screening, regulator interactions, team training.
- **Regulatory AML report.** Per frequency required by AOFA (quarterly/annual).
- **Ad-hoc SAR (Suspicious Activity Report).** To the regulator within regulatory timeframe (typically 3-14 days from detection).
- **Semi-annual internal audit.** Review of approved KYC cases, sampling, findings.

**KPIs measured.**
- KYC pass rate 70-85% (too high = lax, too low = over-compliant friction on revenue).
- AML alerts cleared within SLA (typically 24-48h).
- Freeze rate <2% of active accounts.
- Major regulator findings = 0.
- Average KYC approval time <24h for standard cases.

**Ideal profile.** Legal or financial background. 5+ years in fintech/broker/bank compliance. CAMS certification (Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist) or equivalent. Knows FATF, OFAC, EU 5AMLD, sanctions regimes. Extremely detail-oriented. Ability to say "no" to the business when Sales pushes.

**Reports to.** CEO + Board directly (for regulatory independence).

**Manages.** Junior Compliance Officers, KYC analysts.

**Critical dependencies.** CEO, external legal counsel, Head of Dealing (transaction monitoring), Finance Manager (fund flow).

**Market cost.** $5K-15K/month (LATAM), $10K-25K/month (EU/UAE).

**At NEOMAAA.** Susana holds this role. Critical role covered. Priority: add 1 junior when volume exceeds 500 KYC/month.

**Risk if vacant.** License loss in 90-180 days. Regulatory fines. Corporate account freezes by banks. Impossible to operate a regulated broker without a CCO.

# LAYER 2 — DEPARTMENT HEADS

They turn strategy into execution. Each Head has their own budget, their own team, and a quantifiable quarterly target.

---

## Head of Sales

**Real function.** Owns the number. Meets the monthly FTDs (first time deposits) and revenue target. Designs the commercial pitch, call script, objections (see [Broker Objections](/en/sales/objections-broker)). Trains the sales team on product, technique, compliance. Assigns territories and leads. Closes important deals personally (VIP onboarding, high-ticket clients). Coordinates with Marketing on lead flow quality.

**Reports generated.**
- **Sales weekly.** FTDs achieved, total deposits, conversion funnel (lead → MQL → SQL → FTD), average ticket, agent-by-agent leaderboard.
- **Weekly pipeline review.** Open deals, month/quarter forecasting, risks.
- **Training gaps report.** Areas where the team fails, coaching plan.

**KPIs measured.**

| KPI | Realistic stage 1 target |
|-----|---------------------------|
| FTDs/month | 50-150 (by stage and market) |
| Monthly deposit volume | $200K-800K |
| Lead → FTD conversion | >15% |
| Average FTD ticket | $500-1,500 |
| 30-day retention | >55% |
| 90-day retention | >35% |
| Win rate per agent | >20% |

**Ideal profile.** Top closer with 5+ years at a broker or prop firm. Verifiable track record of high-ticket consultative sales. Multi-language (minimum ES+EN). Coaching ability — knowing how to sell is NOT the same as teaching how to sell. Charismatic but disciplined with CRM.

**Reports to.** CEO or COO.

**Manages.** Sales Agents (3-10 in stage 1-2), Team Leads per market in stage 3.

**Critical dependencies.** Marketing (lead quality), Compliance (fast KYC), Support (post-sale retention), CRM/Tech (working Skale).

**Market cost.** $4K-12K/month base + team variable commission (1-3% of net revenue generated). Total OTE: $8K-25K.

**At NEOMAAA.** Edward functions as top closer + informal lead. The role needs to be formalized with a dedicated Head and backup plan. Franco and Luis are internal candidates to consider.

**When to hire.** Stage 1 day 1. One of the first 3 essential hires post-launch.

**Risk if vacant.** Inconsistent revenue. Sales agents without direction. Blind pipeline. The broker burns cash without generating enough FTDs.

**KPIs measured.**

| KPI | Target |
|-----|----------|

| Email FRT | <2h |
| CSAT | >85% |
| Median ticket resolution time | <4h |
| Escalation rate to L2 | <5% |

**Ideal profile.** 3-5 years in fintech/broker customer support. Intercom + Zendesk + CRM mastery. Minimum bilingual (ES/EN or EN/RU). Experience leading remote multi-shift teams. Data-driven.

**Reports to.** COO.

**Manages.** ES + EN Support Agents (4-8 people in stage 2).

**Critical dependencies.** Head of Dealing (technical escalations), Compliance (KYC cases), Finance (deposit/withdrawal issues), Tech (portal bugs).

**Market cost.** $3K-8K/month.

## Head of Marketing

Executes and supervises Meta/Google/TikTok/YouTube/Email campaigns. Manages content strategy, social media, PR. Coordinates with Sales to guarantee lead quality. Designs retention: email flows, lifecycle marketing, gamification.

**Reports generated.**

**KPIs measured.**
- CAC <$150 in stage 1, <$250 in stage 2 (depends on market and ticket).
- Monthly lead volume per FTD target (ratio 10-15 leads / 1 FTD).
- Lead → FTD >15%.
- 30/60/90 retention: 55%/45%/35%.
- Content engagement rate >3% on social.
- Brand search volume +20% quarter-over-quarter.

**Ideal profile.** 5-8 years in regulated fintech performance marketing (critical — not every marketer understands broker ad restrictions). Personally manages Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, TikTok Ads, Email tools. Analytical (GA4, Mixpanel, Amplitude). Bilingual ES/EN. Experience in creative briefs + data-driven optimization.

**Reports to.** CEO or COO.

**Manages.** Marketing Specialists (performance, content, SEO), Designer, Copywriter.

**Critical dependencies.** Head of Sales (lead quality), Compliance (claims allowed in ads), CFO (budget and ROI).

**Market cost.** $4K-10K/month (LATAM), $8K-18K/month (EU/UAE).

**At NEOMAAA.** Vacant. Critical for post-launch. Can start with senior freelance + full-time specialist, and scale to Head when budget is consistent >$30K/month in paid media.

## Head of Dealing

**Real function.** Correct and profitable order execution. Manages the broker's book: exposure per instrument, A-Book vs B-Book decisions (see [A-Book/B-Book Policy](/en/compliance/ab-book-policy)). Negotiates and maintains relationships with liquidity providers and bridges. Monitors slippage, requotes, execution quality. Protects the broker's P&L. Detects and neutralizes abuse (scalping, arbitrage, toxic flow).

**Reports generated.**
- **Dealing daily.** Total volume, broker P&L, net exposure per top 10 instruments, incidents.
- **Risk weekly.** Estimated VaR, hedge ratios, LP performance, top traders by exposure.
- **Monthly review.** B-Book hit ratio, A-Book routing cost, execution quality benchmarks.

**KPIs measured.**
- Median execution speed <150ms.
- Average slippage <0.3 pips on majors.
- Broker P&L net positive month over month.
- B-Book hit ratio 70-80% (too high = concentrated risk, too low = not leveraging the model).
- LP costs as % of volume <0.005%.
- Toxic flow identified and migrated to A-Book in <7 days.

**Ideal profile.** 5+ years as a dealer at a retail broker or market maker. Knows advanced MT5 admin, FIX protocol, liquidity aggregation, bridge config (oneZero, PrimeXM, Brokeree). Ideally ex-bank or institutional. Risk management discipline. Cold under pressure.

**Reports to.** CEO or COO.

**Manages.** Dealing Operators (24/5 shifts), Risk Analysts in stage 3.

**Critical dependencies.** CTO/Tech (execution infra), Compliance (market abuse), CFO (P&L and capital).

**Market cost.** $6K-15K/month (LATAM), $12K-25K/month (EU/UAE).

**At NEOMAAA.** Pepe holds this role (20 years of experience). Critical role covered. Plan: hire 1-2 Dealing Operators to cover shifts and back up Pepe.

**Risk if vacant.** Broker P&L in chaos. Unfiltered toxic flow destroys the book in weeks. Execution incidents without fast response = clients lose money and complain.

## Finance Manager

**Real function.** Day-to-day financial operation. Daily reconciliation between Skale CRM, PSPs, and bank accounts. Processes deposits and withdrawals with Compliance. Payments to partners, employees, vendors. Operating cashflow. Right hand of the CFO (or the CEO if no CFO). Owner of daily financial data.

**Reports generated.**
- **Daily reconciliation report.** Match between CRM/PSP/bank, discrepancies, resolutions.
- **Weekly cashflow.** Position per account, 30-day projection, pending withdrawals.
- **Monthly preliminary P&L.** Raw data for the CFO to close.

**KPIs measured.**
- Reconciliation accuracy 100% (zero tolerance for errors).
- On-time payments 100% (employees, partners, client withdrawals).
- Monthly days-to-close <5.
- PSP cost % of volume optimized quarter over quarter.
- Average withdrawal processing time <24h.

**Ideal profile.** Accountant (CPA or local equivalent) with 3-5 years in fintech/broker. Comfort with multi-currency, multi-PSP. Advanced Excel, familiarity with NetSuite/Xero. Disciplined, detail-oriented, with good communication to escalate issues.

**Reports to.** CFO (when exists) or CEO directly.

**Manages.** Finance Junior (in stage 2+).

**Critical dependencies.** Compliance (large withdrawal approvals), Head of Dealing (broker P&L), Support (deposit/withdrawal tickets).

**Market cost.** $3K-8K/month.

**At NEOMAAA.** Vacant. Critical hire #1 per [Finance Manager JD](/en/hiring/finance-manager). Without this role, withdrawals get delayed and reconciliation breaks.

**When to hire.** Stage 1 day 1. A broker cannot operate without a Finance Manager.

**Risk if vacant.** Delayed withdrawals = complaints and negative reviews. Broken reconciliation = undetected money loss. PSPs cut service over unresolved disputes.

# LAYER 3 — OPERATIONAL ROLES

The daily execution force. 60-75% of total headcount.

---

## Sales Agent

**Real function.** Converts leads into FTDs. Runs welcome calls (fresh lead 0-24h), retention calls (client post-first deposit), upselling (increase deposit or migrate to premium account). Follows the [Contact Plan](/en/sales/plan-contacto). Logs everything in CRM (Skale). Escalates technical or KYC issues to the right team.

**Reports generated.**
- **Daily activity report.** Calls made, effective contacts, conversions, closed deposits.
- **Weekly closing report.** Agent FTDs, deposit volume, contacts/FTD ratio.

**KPIs measured.**
- Dials/day >80.
- Effective contacts reached/day >30.
- FTDs/month: 5-15 by level (junior vs senior).
- Lead-to-FTD conversion rate >15%.
- Monthly deposit volume per quota.
- 30d retention of their own FTDs >50%.

**Ideal profile.** Persuasive, high-energy, resilient (80% rejection). Multi-language per assigned market. No prior broker experience OK if they have a base in high-ticket phone sales of any industry (insurance, real estate, forex, prop firms). Discipline with CRM.

**Reports to.** Head of Sales (or Team Lead in stage 3).

**Critical dependencies.** Marketing (lead quality), Compliance (fast KYC), Support (post-sale handoff).

**Market cost.** $1.5K-4K/month base + 5-15% commission on net revenue generated. Top agents reach $6K-12K OTE in LATAM.

**At NEOMAAA.** Franco, Luis, Rocio, Marilyn. Plan: scale to 6-8 agents in the first 6 months post-launch.

**Risk if team is insufficient.** FTD target not met. Leads burn out from slow response (more than 10 min from signup).

## Support Agent

**Real function.** Handles client tickets and chats. Resolves deposit/withdrawal topics (level 1), KYC (level 1), basic technical issues (login, password, platform). Follows scripts from [Support Encyclopedia](/en/support/enciclopedia-soporte). Escalates to L2 (Finance/Compliance/Dealing/Tech) what they can't resolve.

**Reports generated.**
- **Daily ticket log.** Tickets resolved, types, times, escalations.

**KPIs measured.**
- Tickets resolved/day >40.
- Chat FRT <5 min.
- Individual CSAT >85%.
- Escalation rate <8%.
- Schedule adherence >95%.

**Ideal profile.** Empathetic, patient, basic technical knowledge (internet, browsers, apps). **Official customer-service languages at NEOMAAA Markets: ES / EN / PT** (confirmed on neomaaa.com). Priority profiles:
- **Native ES** + intermediate EN minimum (Spanish-speaking LATAM coverage + Spain compliance backoffice).
- **Native PT (Brazil)** + intermediate EN minimum — critical because Brazil (BMP) is a priority market.
- **Native EN** + second language (ES or PT preferred, or Russian/Arabic for CIS/MENA coverage).

Handles Intercom/Zendesk. Multitasking capability. Internal portal and operational materials: ES primary. Translations roadmap: ES → RU → EN (PT deferred to a later phase). Brazil operational coverage with a dedicated PT-BR agent.

**Reports to.** Head of Support (or Team Lead).

**Critical dependencies.** Finance (deposits/withdrawals), Compliance (KYC), Dealing (trading technical issues), Tech (platform bugs).

**Market cost.** $1.2K-3K/month.

**At NEOMAAA.** 2 vacancies open (ES + EN) — see [Support EN JD](/en/hiring/support-en) and [Support ES JD](/en/hiring/support-es).

**When to hire.** Stage 1 day 1 (minimum 2). Scale per volume: general rule 1 agent per 500 active clients.

**Risk if team is insufficient.** SLAs break. Churn increases. Negative public reviews.

## Compliance Officer (Junior)

**Real function.** Daily KYC review (document validation, sanctions screening, enhanced due diligence mid-risk cases). AML alerts triage. Sanctions lists updates. Support to CCO on regulatory reports. Documentation of decisions for audit trail.

**Reports generated.**
- **Daily KYC stats.** Reviewed, approved, rejected, pending.
- **AML alerts log.** Alerts received, triaged, escalated, resolved.

**KPIs measured.**
- KYC backlog <24h.
- KYC decisions accuracy >98% (measured by CCO audit).
- AML alerts cleared within SLA >95%.
- Zero sanctioned cases approved.

**Ideal profile.** Legal or financial background, 1-3 years experience. English required. Extreme attention to detail. Able to say "no" to business when Sales pushes to approve.

**Reports to.** CCO (Susana).

**Critical dependencies.** Head of Sales (onboarding), Finance Manager (suspicious withdrawals).

**Market cost.** $2K-5K/month.

**At NEOMAAA.** Susana covers. Add 1 junior when sustained KYC volume exceeds 500/month.

## Dealing Operator

**Real function.** Monitors execution 24/5. Resolves platform issues in real time. Manual price adjustments (requotes, extreme slippage). Escalates major incidents to Head of Dealing. Covers night shifts/Asia weekends.

**Reports generated.**
- **Shift report.** Anomalous executions, issues, exposure changes, escalations.

**KPIs measured.**
- Shift coverage uptime (never no dealer on shift).
- Issues resolved on shift >90%.
- Incidents escalated with RCA documented 100%.

**Ideal profile.** Ex-retail trader with platform knowledge, or junior dealer with 1-2 years. Comfort with rotating shifts including overnight. English required. Cold blood.

**Reports to.** Head of Dealing (Pepe).

**Market cost.** $2K-5K/month.

**At NEOMAAA.** Pepe covers alone. Hire when volume requires 3-shift coverage (approximately stage 2, 6-12 months post-launch).

**Reports generated.**

**KPIs measured.**

- ROAS >2x in stage 1.

**Ideal profile.** 2-4 years performance marketing. Meta/Google certifications. Ideally fintech or regulated-ads experience. Analytical.

## Customer Success Manager (CSM) — VIP

**Real function.** Manages portfolio of VIP/institutional clients (deposits >$10K). Monthly proactive calls, quarterly reviews, expectation management. Detects early churn signals. Coordinates special privileges (execution priority, rebates, dedicated account manager).

**Reports generated.**
- **Monthly VIP portfolio status.** Deposits, withdrawals, activity, churn risk.
- **Quarterly retention initiatives.**

**KPIs measured.**
- 12-month VIP retention >85%.
- VIP NPS >60.
- Incremental VIP deposits >10% QoQ.
- Zero-contact days per VIP <30.

**Ideal profile.** Empathetic, professional, multilingual. Background in wealth management, private banking or VIP broker desk. Comfort with 1-on-1 Zoom calls.

**Market cost.** $3K-7K/month.

**At NEOMAAA.** Pepe covers informally. Create formal role when active VIPs >50.

# LAYER 4 — EXTERNAL ROLES (OUTSOURCEABLE)

Not hired full-time. Monthly retainer or project.

## Legal Counsel

**Real function.** Review T&Cs, client agreements, contracts with partners/PSPs/LPs. Disputes with clients. Regulatory interpretation. M&A due diligence.

**Frequency.** On-demand, monthly retainer.

**Market cost.** $2K-8K/month retainer. $150-500/hour ad-hoc.

**NEOMAAA recommendation.** Have a retainer with a legal firm specialized in offshore jurisdictions from day 1. Minimum 5 hours/month retainer.

---

## External Auditor

**Real function.** Audits annual financials. Required by regulator (AOFA) and for due diligence of serious PSPs/partners.

**Frequency.** Annual (audit), semi-annual (review).

**Market cost.** $5K-30K/year by broker size.

**NEOMAAA recommendation.** Hire a firm experienced with offshore brokers (Baker Tilly, Nexia, BDO regional offices). First recommended audit: end of year 1 of operation.

---

## External Accountant (multi-jurisdiction)

**Real function.** Monthly/quarterly/annual tax filings in each operational jurisdiction (Anjouan, holding jurisdictions, LATAM employees).

**Frequency.** Monthly + annual closing.

**Market cost.** $1K-5K/month.

**NEOMAAA recommendation.** Local accountants in each relevant jurisdiction, coordinated by the CFO.

---

## IT Security Consultant

**Real function.** Annual pen testing. Security audit. Incident response in case of breach. IT compliance (SOC2, ISO 27001 if certified).

**Frequency.** Annual scheduled + ad-hoc on-incident.

**Market cost.** $5K-20K per pen test project. $150-400/hour incident response.

**NEOMAAA recommendation.** Hire pen test in year 2 of operation, annual thereafter. Incident response retainer with defined contact from day 1.
