# Job Description — Support Agent (Spanish/LATAM)

**Company:** Neomaaa Ltd (IBC 15968) | License L15968/N (AOFA, Anjouan)
**Position:** Support Agent — Spanish/LATAM
**Mode:** Remote (must reside in a LATAM time zone)
**Compensation:** $400 USD/month
**Hours:** Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Colombia/Mexico time)
**Reports to:** Head of Support / Principals
**Start:** Immediate

---

## Role Overview

The Support Agent (Spanish) is the first point of contact for NEOMAAA Markets' Spanish-speaking clients. They handle queries via Intercom (live chat), email and WhatsApp. Their function is to resolve questions, guide clients through registration, KYC, deposits and withdrawals, and escalate technical or compliance topics to the corresponding team. They are the voice of the broker for the **LATAM market** (Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Colombia, Chile, Peru and the rest — excluding USA/Canada).

> [!WARNING]
> **Does NOT serve clients residing in Spain / EEA / UK** — those are RESTRICTED jurisdictions for NEOMAAA Markets. If a Spanish client makes contact, respond with the CR-26 rejection template for jurisdictional restriction (do not offer workarounds). Spanish peninsular support is not a target until NEOMAAA obtains an equivalent EEA license.

---

## Responsibilities

### Customer Service
1. Answer client queries in Spanish via Intercom (live chat), email and WhatsApp within established timeframes.
2. Resolve frequent questions about account types (Cent, Standard, Raw, Institutional), trading conditions, spreads, commissions, and leverage.
3. Guide new clients through registration, identity verification (KYC via Sumsub), and MetaTrader 5 setup.

### Deposits and Withdrawals
4. Inform about available deposit methods (120+ methods) and guide the process step by step.
5. Answer queries about deposit and withdrawal status, checking Skale CRM when necessary.
6. Escalate payment issues to the Finance Manager or the corresponding PSP.

### KYC and Compliance
7. Guide clients through identity verification via Sumsub.
8. Explain documentation requirements and resolve questions about document rejections.
9. Escalate compliance cases to the Compliance Officer when necessary.

### Basic Technical Support
10. Assist with basic MetaTrader 5 issues: download, installation, login, chart configuration.
11. Escalate advanced technical issues (execution, server connectivity, platform errors) to the technical team.

### Information Management
12. Record every interaction in the system (Intercom/CRM) with clear notes on the problem and resolution.
13. Identify frequently asked questions and suggest improvements to internal FAQ and help materials.
14. Report recurring complaint or issue patterns to the supervisor.
