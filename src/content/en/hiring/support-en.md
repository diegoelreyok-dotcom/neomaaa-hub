# Job Description — Support Agent (English / MENA-Africa-Asia)

**Company:** Neomaaa Ltd (IBC 15968) | License L15968/N (AOFA, Anjouan)
**Position:** Support Agent — English (MENA / Africa / Asia)
**Mode:** Remote (must reside in a CET/GST-compatible time zone)
**Compensation:** $400 USD/month
**Hours:** Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM CET (reference only — does NOT imply EEA clients)
**Reports to:** Head of Support / Principals
**Start:** Immediate

> [!WARNING]
> This role does **NOT serve EEA/UK/Australia/USA/Canada clients** — those are restricted jurisdictions. CET hours are for operational convenience (overlap with MENA and early Asia Pacific). Any EEA/UK lead that comes in by mistake is rejected with the CR-26 template. English support covers: MENA (UAE, Saudi, Qatar, Turkey excl. Iraq), Africa (excl. Sudan), Asia phase 2 (excl. NK/Myanmar), and LATAM clients who prefer English.

---

## Role Overview

The Support Agent (English) is the first point of contact for NEOMAAA Markets' English-speaking clients, covering the **operational markets of MENA, Africa and Asia (phase 2)**. They handle queries via Intercom (live chat), email and WhatsApp. Their function is to resolve questions, guide clients through registration, KYC, deposits and withdrawals, and escalate technical or compliance topics to the corresponding team. They cover the CET/GST shift to ensure support coverage during the peak activity hours of these markets. **Europe (EEA) and UK are NOT operational markets** — if a client from those jurisdictions contacts us, reject with CR-26 template.

---

## Responsibilities

### Customer Service
1. Answer client queries in English via Intercom (live chat), email and WhatsApp within established timeframes.
2. Resolve frequent questions about account types (Cent, Standard, Raw, Institutional), trading conditions, spreads, commissions, and leverage.
3. Guide new clients through registration, identity verification (KYC via Sumsub), and MetaTrader 5 setup.

### Deposits and Withdrawals
4. Inform about available deposit methods (120+ methods) and guide the process step by step.
5. Answer queries about deposit and withdrawal status, checking Skale CRM when necessary.
6. Escalate payment issues to the Finance Manager or the corresponding PSP.

### KYC and Compliance
7. Guide clients through identity verification via Sumsub.
8. Explain documentation requirements and resolve questions about document rejections.
9. Escalate compliance cases to the Compliance Officer when necessary.

### Basic Technical Support
10. Assist with basic MetaTrader 5 issues: download, installation, login, chart configuration.
11. Escalate advanced technical issues (execution, server connectivity, platform errors) to the technical team.

### Information Management
12. Record every interaction in the system (Intercom/CRM) with clear notes on the problem and resolution.
13. Identify frequently asked questions and suggest improvements to internal FAQ and help materials.
14. Report recurring complaint or issue patterns to the supervisor.

### Regional Coverage
15. Understand the particularities of Middle East and Africa markets (local payment methods, cultural sensitivities, basic local regulations).
16. Coordinate with the Support Agent (Spanish) to ensure a clean shift handover and continuity on open tickets.

## Requirements

### Required
- Fluent English (written and spoken — minimum C1 level). English is the primary language of this position.
- Minimum 1 year of customer service experience (any industry).
- Availability to cover the full 9:00–18:00 CET schedule.
- Stable internet connection and functional computer equipment.
- Ability to learn new tools quickly (training provided on MT5, Skale CRM, Sumsub, Intercom).
- Service-oriented attitude, patience, and clear communication.

### Preferred
- Basic understanding of forex and trading (what a pip is, what leverage is, what a spread is).
- Prior experience in forex broker, fintech, or trading platform support.
- Familiarity with MetaTrader 5 (NEOMAAA operates only on MT5). Prior MT4 experience appreciated.
- Experience with Intercom or similar live-chat tools.
- Arabic as a second language (significant advantage for Middle East market).
- Residence in Eastern Europe (for the operational time zone — NOT for serving EEA clients), Turkey, Egypt, South Africa, UAE, or countries with CET/GST-compatible time zones.
- Basic Spanish (for internal communication with part of the team).
