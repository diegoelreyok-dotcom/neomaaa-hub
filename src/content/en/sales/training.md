## Why this program exists

Selling broker is NOT selling propfirm. Product, client, regulatory risk and retention cycle all change. An agent who sells broker with a propfirm mindset within 48h: promises returns (illegal), can't explain spreads/swap/margin, ignores compliance, and loses clients by not handling trust objections.

| Propfirm | Broker |
|----------|--------|
| Client pays challenge (one-time fee) | Client deposits their real money |
| Propfirm's risk | Client's risk |
| KPI: challenges sold | KPI: FTDs + retention + recurring deposits |
| Lax regulation | Mandatory KYC/AML |
| Sale ends at the challenge | Sale begins long-term relationship |

## WEEK 1 — Broker Product: the fundamentals

**Instructor:** Pepe
**Goal:** every agent understands the product as if they were a trader.

**Mandatory reading (before the week):**
- [`encyclopedia/abc`](/content/encyclopedia/abc) — full glossary
- [`encyclopedia/productos-mt5`](/content/encyclopedia/productos-mt5) — especially "Account types" section (gold source) + instruments + leverage + contract specs

**Topics per day:**
- **Day 1:** What a broker is, how it makes money, broker vs propfirm differences. Spreads: what they are, why they vary.
- **Day 2:** Leverage, margin, free margin, margin call, stop out, negative balance protection. Numeric examples.
- **Day 3:** Swaps, Raw vs Standard commissions, Swap-free, hidden costs clients ask about.
- **Day 4:** Instruments (2,000+): forex majors/minors/exotics, indices, metals (XAU), crypto CFDs, stocks, ETFs.
- **Day 5:** NEOMAAA vs competition (spreads, leverage, LATAM payment methods). What to say/NOT say about competitors.

**Practical exercises (the trainee MUST do):**
1. Calculate total cost (spread + commission) of 1 lot EURUSD on each account type.
2. Simulate $500 trade with 1:500, calculate required margin and stop out point.
3. Calculate overnight swap for 1 lot EURUSD held for 1 week.
4. Build a "recommended portfolio" for a typical LATAM client.

**Week 1 exam:** 30 written questions. Minimum grade: **80%**.

## WEEK 2 — MetaTrader 5: hands-on

**Instructor:** Pepe
**Goal:** every agent navigates MT5, opens/closes trades, provides basic platform support.

**Mandatory reading:**
- [`encyclopedia/productos-mt5`](/content/encyclopedia/productos-mt5) — order types, contract specs, access platforms, common errors
- [`support/enciclopedia-soporte`](/content/support/enciclopedia-soporte) — troubleshooting and trader glossary

**Topics per day:**
- **Day 1:** Download/install MT5 desktop + mobile. Navigation: Market Watch, Chart, Navigator, Terminal, Toolbox.
- **Day 2:** Orders (market, buy/sell limit, buy/sell stop). Modify SL/TP. Partial/full close.
- **Day 3:** Charts, timeframes, basic indicators (MA, RSI, MACD, Bollinger), templates.
- **Day 4:** Account management: history, balance/equity/margin, how to generate statement.
- **Day 5:** Common troubleshooting — "won't open", "closed by itself", password, EA install.

**Practical exercises:**
1. Configure MT5 with personal layout.
2. Open 5 trades of different types, modify, close.
3. Prepare a presentable chart to show a prospect.
4. Generate a demo account statement and explain each line.

**Week 2 exam:** Practical demo — open, manage, and close 10 trades with verbal explanation.

## WEEK 3 — Compliance: zero regulatory risk

**Instructor:** Susana
**Goal:** the team does NOT put NEOMAAA at regulatory risk.

**Mandatory reading (blocking — no exam without this):**
- [`compliance/manual-susana`](/content/compliance/manual-susana) — principles + public vs confidential A-Book/B-Book
- [`compliance/frases-prohibidas`](/content/compliance/frases-prohibidas) — **gold source** of 28 prohibited phrases + 14 approved + 5 disclaimers. Memorize.
- [`compliance/screening-sanciones`](/content/compliance/screening-sanciones) section 12.5 — restricted countries (gold source)
- [`compliance/risk-matrix`](/content/compliance/risk-matrix) — LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH framework (gold source)

**Topics per day:**
- **Day 1:** Legal identity Neomaaa Ltd / IBC 15968 / L15968/N / AOFA. Execution-only basis. Restricted countries — gold source in screening-sanciones 12.5. Real consequences of violating compliance.
- **Day 2:** PROHIBITED phrases (all 28 — memorize) and their approved alternatives. Gold source: frases-prohibidas.
- **Day 3:** Mandatory disclaimers (short / long / execution-only / counterparty / leverage). How/when to insert them. Gold source: frases-prohibidas section 3.
- **Day 4:** KYC: sales agent role. Sumsub flow. How to act on GREEN/YELLOW/RED/PEP/Sanctions hit. 3-category framework (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH) in risk-matrix.
- **Day 5:** Roleplay 10 scenarios — client asks for guarantees, asks A/B-Book, wants to know if they're being traded against.

**Practical exercises:**
1. Memorize and recite from memory the 28 prohibited phrases with their alternatives.
2. Answer without hesitation the full legal identity.
3. Roleplay where client asks for an "entry signal" and agent redirects without violating compliance.
4. Identify a restricted country in 5 edge cases (dual nationality, EEA residency, US IP with LATAM passport).

**Week 3 exam:** 25 written questions + 3 roleplays evaluated by Susana.
**Minimum grade: 90%.** Failing = repeat the full week. Zero tolerance for compliance errors.

## WEEK 4 — The art of selling broker

**Instructor:** Diego
**Goal:** pitch, objections, broker-specific closes.

**Mandatory reading:**
- [`sales/objections-broker`](/content/sales/objections-broker) — full objections playbook
- [`sales/faq-ventas`](/content/sales/faq-ventas) — approved answers
- [`sales/primer-contacto`](/content/sales/primer-contacto) — opening scripts
- [`sales/plan-contacto`](/content/sales/plan-contacto) — cadence and channels

**Topics per day:**
- **Day 1:** 3-minute pitch structure — Hook / Pain / Solution / Social proof / CTA.
- **Day 2:** The 15 most common objections and their approved answers. Objections map from objections-broker.
- **Day 3:** Closing techniques — assumed, alternative, time, minor commitment.
- **Day 4:** Channels — WhatsApp templates, call (8-12 min ideal), email. 5-min rule on a hot lead.
- **Day 5:** Follow-up cadence (day 0, 1, 3, 7, 14) and criteria to move to nurture.

**Practical exercises:**
1. Each agent records a 3-minute pitch and it's reviewed as a group.
2. Close 5 simulated calls evaluated by Diego.
3. Write 3 WhatsApp sequences (hot, warm, cold).
4. Handle the top 15 objections in roleplay.

**Week 4 exam:** Recorded call evaluated (pitch + 3 objections + close attempt) + written objections exam.

**Week 5 exam:** Execute full flow live (fictitious lead → contact → simulated KYC → FTD).

## WEEK 6 — Final simulations and certification

**Instructor:** Diego + Susana + Pepe
**Goal:** final evaluation — only those who demonstrate mastery pass.

**Topics per day:**
- **Day 1-2:** 10 simulated calls per agent with profiles: beginner, experienced, distrustful, wants to deposit more than reasonable, restricted country, asks for investment advice, rejected KYC, frustrated with withdrawal, potential VIP ($10K+), referral.
- **Day 3:** **Final written exam** (50 questions): Product 15, MT5 10, Compliance 15, Sales 10. Passing: 85% overall, **95% in compliance**.
- **Day 4:** **Final practical exam** — 15-min recorded call with external evaluator.

| Criterion | Weight | Pass | Fail |
|-----------|--------|------|------|
| Compliance (nothing prohibited) | 30% | 0 errors | 1+ errors |
| Product knowledge | 25% | Explains correctly | Factual errors |
| Objection handling | 20% | Answers 4/5+ | Less than 3/5 |
| Close | 15% | Attempts close 2x+ | Doesn't attempt |
| Professionalism | 10% | Clear, confident | Informal, insecure |

- **Day 5:** Results. Those who don't pass: 1 week of remediation. Those who pass: internal NEOMAAA certification + day 1 lead assignment + kickoff.
