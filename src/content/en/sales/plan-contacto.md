# Contact Plan — Stage 1

## Goal: Activate the ~250 Registered Users

> Edward leads the initial outreach to the ~250 users already registered on the platform. Once he masters the process, he trains Franco and Luis to scale.

---

## Context

- We have ~250 people already registered at neomaaa.com
- Many come from the NEOMAAA prop trading ecosystem — they already know the brand
- The goal is to move them from "registered" to "KYC completed + first deposit (FTD)"
- Edward starts solo, learns, documents, and then trains Franco and Luis

---

## Phase 1: Preparation (Days 1-3)

### Edward must have before making the first call:

- [ ] Full access to Skale CRM with the 250 leads loaded
- [ ] Access to MT5 admin to verify account status
- [ ] Approved call script (see below)
- [ ] Access to Sumsub to verify each lead's KYC status
- [ ] Broker FAQ list (spreads, deposits, withdrawals, leverage)
- [ ] Basic knowledge of the 4 account types (Cent, Standard, Raw, Institutional)
- [ ] Access to the operations portal (this site)

### Classification of the 250 registered users

Before calling, Edward must classify each lead in Skale CRM:

| Category | Definition | Priority |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| **A — Hot** | Recent registration (last 30 days), KYC completed or deposit attempt | HIGH — call first |
| **B — Warm** | Registered 30-90 days ago, incomplete KYC or no deposit | MEDIUM — call week 1-2 |
| **C — Cold** | Registered 90+ days ago, no activity | LOW — contact via email/WhatsApp first |
| **D — Prop Crossover** | Comes from NEOMAAA prop trading, already knows us | HIGH — personalized message |

## Phase 3: Edward Trains Franco and Luis (Days 12-20)

> Edward starts training while still doing outreach. He doesn't stop his activity — dedicates 1-2 hours/day to training.

### Training week (5 days)

| Day | Topic | Method |
|-----|-------|--------|
| **Day 1** | Product: account types, MT5, instruments, deposits | Edward presents + Franco/Luis navigate the platform |
| **Day 2** | CRM: how to use Skale, classify leads, log notes, schedule follow-ups | Hands-on in CRM with real data |
| **Day 3** | Scripts + Objections: practice calls, handle "not interested", "I already have a broker", etc. | Role-play: Edward plays the client, they sell |
| **Day 4** | Compliance: what NOT to say, prohibited phrases, how to handle KYC | Susana joins 30 min, then practice |
| **Day 5** | Supervised live calls: Franco and Luis call, Edward listens and gives feedback | Real calls to B/C leads |

### After training

- Franco and Luis start with B and C leads (easier, less risk)
- Edward retains A and D leads (hotter, need experience)
- Daily 15-min meeting (17:00): numbers, blockers, what to improve
- Edward reviews the first 10 CRM notes from each to validate quality

## Handling Common Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I already have another broker" | "Perfect — many of our traders use 2-3 brokers. Why not try with a Cent account of $5? No commitment, and you can compare spreads and execution." |
| "I don't have money right now" | "I understand. The Cent account starts from $5, literally the price of a coffee. It's for testing, not for risking." |
| "I don't trust new brokers" | "NEOMAAA isn't new — we already run prop trading with thousands of traders. Now we're expanding to brokerage with regulated license L15968/N. Your funds are segregated." |
| "I need to think about it" | "Totally. Here's my WhatsApp for when you're ready. In the meantime, would you like me to send a quick guide on how the platform works?" |
| "What about my withdrawals?" | "Withdrawals are processed within 24 business hours. Same method you used to deposit. No surprises." |
| "What spread do you have?" | "Depends on the account: Standard from 1.0 pip no commission, Raw from 0.0 pips + $3/lot/side. What instruments do you trade?" |
