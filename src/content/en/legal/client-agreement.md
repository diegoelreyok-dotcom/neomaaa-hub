2.2 This Agreement shall be read together with the General Terms and Conditions, the Risk Disclosure Statement, the Privacy Policy, and the Order Execution Policy, each of which forms an integral part of this Agreement.

## 3. SERVICES PROVIDED

3.1 The Company will provide the Client with the following services:

- Access to the MetaTrader 5 platform (desktop, web, and mobile) for order execution.
- Access to more than 2,000 financial instruments: CFDs on forex, cryptocurrencies, precious metals, shares, indices, ETFs, futures, and energy.
- Deposit and withdrawal services through more than 120 payment methods.
- Copy Trading service for the automated replication of trades.
- Vault Yield System for generating yield on deposited funds.
- Client support through Intercom, email, and telephone.
- Analysis and educational tools available on the platform.

3.2 The Company acts exclusively as a financial intermediary. The Company does NOT provide investment advice, financial advice, portfolio management, or personalized trading recommendations.

## 6. ORDER EXECUTION

6.1 Orders are executed through the MetaTrader 5 platform under a hybrid ECN/STP execution model. Neomaaa Ltd may act as principal or counterparty to the Client's transactions, as set forth in the General Terms and Conditions and in the published Order Execution Policy.

6.2 Orders are executed at the best available price at the time of execution (Market Execution). The Company does not guarantee execution at the exact price requested.

6.3 Slippage (the difference between the requested price and the execution price) may occur, particularly during periods of high volatility or low liquidity, and may be positive or negative.

6.4 The Company reserves the right to reject, modify, or cancel orders in the following circumstances:

- Manifestly erroneous quotation price (error quote).
- Insufficient available margin.
- Exceeding established position limits.
- Suspected market manipulation or platform abuse.
- Extraordinary market conditions.

6.5 For further detail on order execution, see the Order Execution Policy.

---

## 7. MARGIN AND LEVERAGE

7.1 The Client must maintain sufficient margin in their account to sustain open positions.

7.2 Maximum available leverage varies by account type and instrument. The Company reserves the right to modify leverage levels at any time upon prior notice.

7.3 When the margin level falls below the Margin Call level, the Client will be alerted and must either deposit additional funds or close positions.

7.4 When the margin level falls below the Stop Out level, the platform will automatically close the positions with the largest losses until the margin level is restored above the threshold.

7.5 The Client is responsible for monitoring their margin level at all times.

## 8. COSTS AND COMMISSIONS

8.1 The costs applicable to the Client's transactions include:

| Item | Description |
|----------|-----------|
| **Spread** | Difference between the bid and ask price. Variable depending on instrument and market conditions. |
| **Commission** | Applicable on Raw accounts (USD 3/lot/side) and Institutional accounts (negotiable). Cent and Standard accounts have no explicit commission. |
| **Swap** | Charge or credit for positions held overnight. Published on the MT5 platform for each instrument. |
| **Currency conversion** | Applicable when the transaction currency differs from the account base currency. |

8.2 The Company reserves the right to modify costs and commissions upon prior notice to the Client.

8.3 Swap-Free accounts are available upon the Client's request.
