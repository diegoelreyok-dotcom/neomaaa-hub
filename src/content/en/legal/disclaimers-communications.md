## 2. Mandatory risk warning

Every commercial communication related to leveraged-instrument trading must include (in a visible format) the following warning:

> *"CFDs are complex instruments and come with a high risk of losing money rapidly due to leverage. You may lose part or all of your invested capital. Past performance is not indicative of future results. Between 70% and 85% of retail traders lose money trading these instruments. Do not invest money you cannot afford to lose. Neomaaa Ltd is regulated under license L15968/N (AOFA, Union of Comoros)."*

Authorized short-form versions for placements with limited space:

- **Social banner:** *"CFD trading carries high risk. 70–85% of retail traders lose money. Do not invest what you cannot afford to lose."*
- **Email footer:** *"Risk warning: CFD trading is risky. Capital at risk."*
- **Video/reel closing card:** *"Trading involves risk. This is not investment advice."*

Full texts of official per-channel disclaimers (short, long, execution-only, counterparty, leverage) are in `compliance/manual-susana.md` section 4.

## 6. Marketing and advertising

### 6.1 Principles

- **Substantial truth** — do not overstate benefits, do not minimize risks.
- **No outcome promises** — prohibited: "guarantee," "no loss," "safe income."
- **No targeting of vulnerable individuals** — minors, individuals with a history of gambling addiction, vulnerable groups.
- **Comparisons** with competitors only on documented factual grounds.

### 6.2 Prohibited language

The master and binding list of **prohibited phrases** and their **approved replacements** lives exclusively in `compliance/manual-susana.md` section 2. Any variant, paraphrase, or deliberate attempt to produce the same effect is likewise prohibited.

### 6.3 Pre-approval

Advertising campaigns, new landing pages, video scripts, and any creative containing legal disclaimers must be pre-approved by the Compliance Officer before going live. Target SLA: 48–72 business hours.

## 7. Communications with existing clients

- **Transactional** (deposit/withdrawal confirmation, KYC, margin call, stop-out) and **compliance** (EDD, KYC refresh) — no opt-out, legally required.
- **Material contractual changes** — minimum advance notice of 10 business days, except in extraordinary circumstances.
- **Marketing opt-in/opt-out** — targeted advertising requires explicit opt-in; the client may opt out from the portal preferences at any time.
- **Retention:** filing per `compliance/compliance-calendar.md` section 13 (minimum 7 years from account closure).

---

## 8. Crisis communications

An incident involving mass communication (downtime >1h, breach, material regulatory change) follows `operations/manual-crisis.md`. Principles: **fast** (first communication within minutes), **honest** (what happened and what is being done), **coordinated** (a single message from Principals + Compliance Officer), **documented** (timeline + postmortem).
