## 4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

### 4.1 Commission Models

The Company offers the following commission models, which are defined in the individual agreement with each Affiliate:

| Model | Description |
|--------|-----------|
| **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)** | A one-time payment for each Referred Client who completes an FTD meeting the minimum requirements (minimum amount and trading conditions). |
| **Revenue Share** | A recurring commission based on a percentage of the spread or commission generated by Referred Clients. |
| **Hybrid** | A combination of CPA and Revenue Share under negotiated terms. |

### 4.2 Commission Qualification Requirements

For a Referred Client to generate a commission, the Client must:

- Register through the Affiliate's referral link or code.
- Successfully complete KYC verification.
- Make an FTD meeting the minimum amount established in the individual agreement.
- Not be an existing NEOMAAA client (self-referrals are not permitted).
- Not originate from a restricted country.

### 4.3 General Commission Terms

- Commissions are calculated monthly and paid within the first 15 business days of the following month.
- The minimum payment amount is USD 100 (or equivalent).
- Commissions are paid in USD or by the method agreed with the Affiliate.
- The Company reserves the right to withhold commissions where fraudulent or artificial traffic is suspected.

## 7. FRAUDULENT TRAFFIC

### 7.1 Definition

The following are considered fraudulent traffic:

- Referred Clients who are the Affiliate itself or persons directly related to the Affiliate (self-referral).
- Clients generated through prohibited incentives (directly sharing commissions with the client as a deposit incentive).
- Clients generated through false or misleading information.
- Clients intentionally directed from restricted countries.
- Clients making deposits and immediate withdrawals without genuine trading activity (wash trading).
- Use of bots, fake traffic, or artificial lead generation methods.

### 7.2 Consequences

If the Company detects fraudulent traffic:

- The associated commissions will be withheld or cancelled.
- Commissions already paid may be clawed back.
- The affiliate agreement will be terminated immediately.
- The Company reserves the right to pursue legal action.
