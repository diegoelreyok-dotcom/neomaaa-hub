## 1. INTRODUCTION

This policy sets forth the terms under which Neomaaa Ltd (IBC 15968), holder of license L15968/N issued by the Anjouan Offshore Finance Authority (AOFA), will process requests for refunds of deposits and handle chargeback procedures.

---

## 2. GENERAL PRINCIPLES

2.1 Deposits made into NEOMAAA trading accounts are credited for the purpose of funding trading activity in financial instruments.

2.2 Deposited funds are withdrawable at any time through the standard withdrawal process described in the General Terms and Conditions, subject to KYC verification and applicable security procedures.

2.3 The Company distinguishes among:

- **Normal withdrawals:** A Client request to withdraw funds available in the account.
- **Refunds:** The return of a specific deposit due to exceptional circumstances.
- **Chargebacks:** A dispute initiated by the Client through the Client's bank or card issuer.

---

## 3. REFUND POLICY

### 3.1 Circumstances in which a Refund May Be Requested

A refund may be considered under the following circumstances:

| Circumstance | Conditions |
|---------------|------------|
| **Duplicate deposit** | The same deposit was processed twice due to technical error. The duplicated amount is refunded. |
| **Deposit not credited** | The deposit was debited from the Client's account but was not credited to the trading account. Investigated, then credited or refunded. |
| **Account rejected during KYC** | The Client deposited before completing KYC and the account was definitively rejected. The full deposit is refunded. |
| **System error** | A technical error resulted in an incorrect deposit. The difference is corrected or refunded. |
| **Confirmed fraud** | The deposit is determined to have been made fraudulently (stolen identity, compromised card). The Company cooperates with the authorities and the cardholder's bank. |

### 3.2 Circumstances in which No Refund Will Be Granted

No refunds will be granted in the following cases:

- **Trading losses:** Funds used in trades that resulted in losses are not refundable. Trading carries risk and the Client has accepted the Risk Disclosure Statement.
- **Post-deposit regret:** A change of mind after depositing does not constitute a basis for a refund. The Client may withdraw available funds through the standard withdrawal process.
- **Funds already traded:** If deposited funds have been used, in whole or in part, for trading, a refund of the original deposit is not available. The Client may withdraw the available balance.
- **Breach of T&Cs:** Where the Client has breached the Terms and Conditions (false information, prohibited activity, etc.).

### 3.3 Refund Request Procedure

1. The Client submits a formal request by email or through the Client Portal.
2. The request must include: account number, deposit amount, date, payment method used, and reason for the request.
3. The operations team verifies the information and evaluates the request.
4. If approved, the refund is processed via the same payment method used for the original deposit.
5. Processing time depends on the payment method (see section 3.4).
6. The Client receives an email notification with confirmation or a reasoned rejection.

### 3.4 Refund Processing Times

| Payment Method | Processing Time |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Credit/debit card | 5–14 business days (subject to the issuing bank) |
| Bank transfer | 3–7 business days |
| Cryptocurrencies | 1–3 business days |
| Local payment methods (PIX, PSE, etc.) | 3–5 business days |

The indicated timeframes are estimates and depend on the payment provider and the Client's bank.

## 4. CHARGEBACK POLICY

### 4.1 Company Position

The Company considers chargebacks initiated illegitimately to be a violation of the Terms and Conditions and potentially a fraudulent act.

### 4.2 Chargeback Process

When the Company receives notification of a chargeback:

1. **Account freeze:** The Client's account will be frozen immediately to prevent further activity.
2. **Investigation:** The deposit history, trading activity, and communications with the Client will be reviewed.
3. **Documentation:** The Company will gather all relevant evidence to dispute the chargeback before the issuing bank, including:
   - Deposit records.
   - KYC verification confirmation (the Client was verified).
   - Trading history (funds were used).
   - Accepted Terms and Conditions.
   - Withdrawal history.
4. **Dispute:** The Company will submit the evidence to the payment processor and the issuing bank in order to dispute the chargeback.
5. **Resolution:** Depending on the outcome:
   - If the chargeback is resolved in the Company's favor: the account is unfrozen and access is restored.
   - If the chargeback is resolved in the Client's favor: the account balance is adjusted accordingly.

### 4.3 Consequences of Fraudulent Chargebacks

If the Company determines that a chargeback was initiated fraudulently (that is, the Client voluntarily deposited, traded with the funds, and then initiated a chargeback to recover the deposit without returning the profits), the Company reserves the right to:

- Permanently close the Client's account.
- Retain any profits generated with the disputed funds.
- Report the activity to the competent authorities.
- Include the Client on internal prohibited-client lists.
- Pursue legal action to recover the funds.

### 4.4 Chargeback Prevention

The Company implements preventive measures including:

- Rigorous KYC verification before accepting deposits.
- Name matching between the account holder and the payment method.
- Deposit confirmations sent to the Client by email.
- Withdrawal-to-same-method policy.
- Recording of the Client's acceptance of the Terms and Conditions.

## 5. WITHDRAWALS VS. REFUNDS

It is important to distinguish between a withdrawal and a refund:

| Concept | Description | Process |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Withdrawal** | The Client requests to withdraw funds available in the trading account | Standard process through the Client Portal |
| **Refund** | Return of a specific deposit due to exceptional circumstance | Formal request assessed by Operations |

The Company recommends that Clients use the standard withdrawal process as the first option for withdrawing funds.

---

## 6. REFUND CURRENCY

Refunds are processed in the same currency and via the same payment method used for the original deposit. If the original method is not available, the Company will coordinate an alternative method with the Client.

---

## 7. COSTS

7.1 The Company does not charge fees for refunds arising from duplicate deposits or system errors.

7.2 In other cases, processing costs of the payment provider (if any) may be deducted from the refunded amount.

7.3 Costs associated with chargebacks (payment processor fees) may be passed to the Client where the chargeback is determined to be illegitimate.

---

## 8. RECORDKEEPING

All refund requests and chargebacks are recorded in the Company's internal system, including:

- Date of request/notification.
- Amount and payment method.
- Reason for the request.
- Outcome of the investigation.
- Action taken.
- Date of resolution.

These records are retained for a minimum period of 5 years in accordance with AOFA requirements.
