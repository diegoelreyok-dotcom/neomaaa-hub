## 6. Bonus Abuse Prohibition

The following constitute **abuse of the bonus system** and may result in bonus removal, profit cancellation, and account closure:

- **Cross-account hedging** — opening opposing positions across multiple accounts (own or related parties) to generate artificial turnover.
- **Multi-accounting** — registering multiple accounts (own or using false identities) to claim welcome bonuses multiple times.
- **Latency arbitrage** — exploiting price differences between feeds with bots against the broker.
- **Synthetic risk-free strategies** intended solely to reach turnover.
- **Claiming a bonus and withdrawing immediately without intent to trade genuinely.**
- **Collusion with an IB, affiliate, or employee** to obtain an ineligible bonus.

The Company reserves the right to investigate any suspicious pattern and to remove bonuses and profits obtained through abuse, without having to prove bad faith beyond the objective patterns observed.
