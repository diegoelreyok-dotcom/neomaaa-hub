## 1. INTRODUCTION

Neomaaa Ltd (IBC 15968), holder of license L15968/N issued by the Anjouan Offshore Finance Authority (AOFA), is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of its clients, website visitors, and users of its services.

This Privacy Policy describes how the Company collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information, as well as the rights of the data subject.

---

## 2. DATA WE COLLECT

### 2.1 Personal Identification Data

- Full name
- Date of birth
- Nationality
- Identity document number (passport, national ID, driver's license)
- Photograph of the document and biometric selfie
- Residential address
- Telephone number
- Email address

### 2.2 Financial Data

- Banking and payment method information
- Deposit and withdrawal history
- Source of funds (where required by regulation)
- Tax information (where applicable)

### 2.3 Trading Account Data

- Account type and configuration
- Trading history
- Open and closed positions
- Balance, equity, and performance statistics

### 2.4 Technical Data

- IP address and approximate location
- Device type, operating system, and browser
- Cookies and session identifiers
- Access logs and platform activity

### 2.5 Communications Data

- Content of support chats (Intercom)
- Exchanged emails
- Telephone call recordings (where applicable)
- Content of forms and surveys

---

## 3. PURPOSES OF PROCESSING

The Company uses personal data for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Legal and Regulatory Compliance

- Identity verification (KYC) in accordance with AOFA regulations.
- Anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CFT) prevention.
- Screening against international sanctions and politically exposed persons (PEPs).
- Compliance with reporting obligations to the AOFA or other competent authorities.

### 3.2 Service Provision

- Opening, maintenance, and administration of trading accounts.
- Processing of deposits and withdrawals.
- Execution of trades on the MetaTrader 5 platform.
- Client service and query resolution.
- Provision of Copy Trading and Vault Yield services.

### 3.3 Communications

- Sending operational notifications (deposit confirmations, account alerts, platform updates).
- Marketing communications (subject to the Client's prior consent).
- Satisfaction and service-improvement surveys.

### 3.4 Security and Fraud Prevention

- Detection and prevention of fraudulent activity.
- Monitoring of suspicious transactions.
- Protection of the integrity of the platform and systems.

### 3.5 Service Improvement

- Statistical and platform performance analysis.
- Personalization of the user experience.
- Development of new products and services.

## 4. LEGAL BASES FOR PROCESSING

Personal data processing is carried out on the following legal bases:

| Legal Basis | Application |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Legal compliance** | KYC, AML, regulatory reporting |
| **Contractual performance** | Provision of trading services, deposits, withdrawals |
| **Consent** | Marketing communications, non-essential cookies |
| **Legitimate interest** | Fraud prevention, platform security, service improvement |

## 5. DATA SHARING

The Company may share personal data with the following third parties:

### 5.1 Service Providers

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Sumsub | KYC verification | Identity documents, biometric data |
| Skale CRM | Customer relationship management | Contact data, account status |
| MetaQuotes (MT5) | Trading platform | Account and trading data |
| Intercom | Customer support | Contact data, communications history |
| Payment service providers (PSPs) | Deposit and withdrawal processing | Financial data required for the transaction |

### 5.2 Regulatory Authorities

The Company may disclose personal data to the AOFA or other regulatory or judicial authorities where required by law or by court order.

### 5.3 Liquidity Providers

Trading data (anonymized) may be shared with liquidity providers as part of the order execution process.

### 5.4 No-Sale Policy

The Company does NOT sell, rent, or trade clients' personal data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.

---

## 6. STORAGE AND RETENTION

### 6.1 Data Location

Data is stored on secure servers. The Company may use cloud infrastructure providers located in different jurisdictions, ensuring that appropriate security measures are maintained.

### 6.2 Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-------------|---------------------|
| KYC identification data | 5 years after account closure |
| Transaction history | 5 years after the last transaction |
| Support communications | 3 years |
| Activity logs | 2 years |
| Marketing data | Until consent is withdrawn |

### 6.3 Data Destruction

Upon expiry of the retention period, data is securely destroyed (permanent digital deletion or physical destruction of media, as applicable).

---

## 7. DATA SECURITY

The Company implements the following security measures:

- SSL/TLS encryption for all communications.
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest.
- Role-based access controls (least-privilege principle).
- Continuous monitoring of access and suspicious activity.
- Regular backups and disaster recovery plans.
- Staff training in data protection.

## 9. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

The Client has the following rights with respect to personal data:

| Right | Description |
|---------|-----------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of the personal data held by the Company. |
| **Rectification** | Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data. |
| **Erasure** | Request deletion of personal data, subject to legal retention obligations. |
| **Restriction** | Request restriction of the processing of personal data. |
| **Portability** | Request the data in a structured, machine-readable format. |
| **Objection** | Object to processing of data for marketing purposes. |
| **Withdrawal of consent** | Withdraw consent previously granted at any time. |

### 9.1 How to Exercise Your Rights

The Client may exercise such rights by submitting a request through:

- Email: the data protection address published on the website.
- Support chat via Intercom.
- Form available in the Client Portal.

The Company will respond to requests within 30 business days.

---

## 10. MINORS

NEOMAAA's services are not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. The Company does not knowingly collect data from minors. If it is determined that a minor has provided personal data, such data will be promptly deleted.

## 11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

The Company reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy. Changes will be communicated to the Client by email or through the Client Portal. Continued use of the services following such notification constitutes acceptance of the changes.
