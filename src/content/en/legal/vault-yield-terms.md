## 1. Definitions

- **"Vault" / "Vault Yield System"** — System operated by Neomaaa Ltd allowing the client to transfer funds to a dedicated, non-trading wallet in order to earn an annualized yield.
- **"Vault Balance"** — The client's amount held in the Vault, excluding accrued but unpaid yield.
- **"APY"** — Annual Percentage Yield, the annualized yield published for the client's plan.
- **"Flexible plan"** — A plan with no lock-up, withdrawable at any time, with a lower APY.
- **"Locked plan"** — A plan with a lock-up period, not withdrawable before maturity save with penalty, with a higher APY.
- **"Pool"** — The aggregated pool of funds from all clients in the Vault, managed by the Company.

## 3. Eligibility

The client must satisfy **all** of the following conditions:

- Be a registered client with **KYC approved** at Neomaaa Ltd.
- **Reside** in a **non-restricted** jurisdiction (see section 8).
- Be of **legal age** in the client's jurisdiction of residence.
- Not be flagged for **EDD, unapproved PEP, or pending sanctions screening**.
- Not have the account blocked due to a compliance investigation.

The Company reserves the right to **deny access** to the Vault to any client at its discretion, without the need to provide a reason.

## 4. Operation

### 4.1 Transfer to the Vault
The client transfers funds from the trading account to the Vault through the portal. The transfer is **immediate** and carries no fee.

### 4.2 Supported asset
The Vault operates on the supported asset(s) defined by internal policy (typically stablecoins such as USDT or other approved assets). The client must hold the supported asset in the account in order to transfer to the Vault.

### 4.3 Yield calculation
- **Daily accrual** based on the Vault Balance at the daily cut-off established by the Company (typically 00:00 UTC).
- **Formula:** `daily_yield = Vault_Balance × (APY / 365)`.
- **Current APY** published on the product page and in the client portal. Variable, updatable based on market conditions.

### 4.4 Yield payment
- **Flexible plan:** credited at the frequency published on the product page (typically daily or weekly) to the Vault Balance or transferred to the trading account as the client elects.
- **Locked plan:** credited at lock maturity (or per sub-plan) with the option of automatic compounding if the client enables it.

### 4.5 Withdrawal from the Vault
- **Flexible plan:** withdrawable at any time. Transfer to the trading balance is immediate.
- **Locked plan:** withdrawal before maturity triggers a **penalty** (forfeiture of accrued yield plus potentially a fixed fee [DATA]). In cases of documented hardship, the Company may waive the penalty on a case-by-case basis.

## 6. Risks

The client acknowledges and accepts the following risks:

### 6.1 No-guarantee risk
The Vault **is not a regulated bank deposit**. There is no FDIC, NCUA, FSCS, FGD, or other deposit guarantee insurance. In the event of insolvency of Neomaaa Ltd, the client **is a creditor** in the insolvency process under the legal ranking applicable in Anjouan, with no special priority.

### 6.2 Counterparty risk
The Vault pool is deployed in yield strategies that involve counterparties: institutional staking, lending desks, whitelisted DeFi protocols, and proprietary treasury. Each counterparty carries specific default or freeze risk. The Company performs reasonable due diligence but **does not guarantee** the solvency of third parties.

### 6.3 Underlying-asset risk (stablecoins)
Where the asset is a stablecoin (USDT, USDC, or other), there is risk of:
- **De-peg** — partial or total loss of the 1:1 peg to the USD.
- **Regulatory freeze** — the issuer may freeze wallets by court or regulatory order.
- **Issuer collapse** — crypto-native execution risk.

### 6.4 Operational and technical risk
- System failures, hacks, smart contract errors (where applicable), human error.
- Service interruptions — the Company mitigates with enterprise-grade redundancy and a 99.9% uptime target.

### 6.5 Regulatory risk
- Regulatory changes in Anjouan or other jurisdictions may force the **suspension or closure** of the Vault without extended prior notice.
- The Company will use reasonable efforts to return the Balance plus accrued yield to the client, but there may be operational delay.

### 6.6 Yield modification risk
The APY is variable. Past returns **do not** guarantee future returns.

> CFDs are complex instruments and come with a high risk of losing money rapidly due to leverage. You may lose part or all of your invested capital. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

## 7. Abuse and prohibitions

The following are prohibited:

- **Money laundering** — use of the Vault as a laundering vehicle. Triggers SAR.
- **Structuring** — splitting deposits to avoid EDD thresholds. May result in closure of the Vault and reporting to AOFA.
- **Multiple accounts** — a client may maintain only one active Vault position, save for special institutional authorization.
- **External transfer to sanctioned wallets** — withdrawals from the Vault to wallets matching sanctions screening are blocked and reported.
- **Fraud as to source of funds** — funds derived from unlawful activity are liable to hold and reporting.

Violations result in: removal from the Vault, hold for investigation, closure of the trading account, and reporting to authorities.

The Vault is **not offered** to residents of:

## 11. Termination

Upon termination by the Company, the Balance plus accrued yield, net of applicable penalty (if any), will be returned via the original deposit method.
