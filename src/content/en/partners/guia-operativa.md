## Partner Onboarding Flow

### Process Stages

```
Application → Review → Approval → Cellxpert Setup → Welcome → First Payout
```

### Stage 1: Application

The partner candidate completes the application form which includes:

- Personal and company data
- Desired program type (Affiliate, Digital, IB)
- Prior experience as affiliate/IB
- Marketing channels used
- Estimated monthly client volume
- Target countries
- References (optional but valued)

**Owner:** Automated form on the partners website.

### Stage 2: Review

| Criterion | What Is Reviewed | Maximum Time |
|---|---|---|
| Identity | Valid identity document, basic verification | 24 hours |
| Experience | History as affiliate/IB, evidence of traffic or clients | 24 hours |
| Channels | Website, social networks, YouTube channel, email list | 24 hours |
| Compliance | Not on sanctions lists, no fraud history | 48 hours |
| Estimated volume | Realism of projections vs candidate profile | 24 hours |

**Owner:** Edward and operations team.

### Stage 3: Approval

| Partner Type | Who Approves | SLA |
|---|---|---|
| Affiliate Partner (any tier) | Edward / team | 48 hours from complete application |
| Digital Affiliate (any tier) | Edward / team | 48 hours from complete application |
| IB Bronze / Silver | Edward / team | 48 hours from complete application |
| IB Senior | Principals | 72 hours from complete application |
| IB Elite | Principals | 72 hours from complete application |
| Any partner with more than 200 existing clients | Principals | 72 hours |

**Rule:** Any IB Senior+ approval requires prior video call with at least one Principal.

### Stage 4: Cellxpert Setup

Once approved, the team configures the partner account in Cellxpert:

1. **Create partner account** in Cellxpert with verified data
2. **Assign program type** (Affiliate, Digital, IB)
3. **Configure initial tier** and corresponding commission rules
4. **Generate unique tracking links** for the partner
5. **Configure sub-affiliates/sub-IBs** if applicable (enable levels per program)
6. **Activate pixel/postback** for Digital Affiliates using media buying
7. **Verify integration** with MT5 so trading activity is reported correctly
8. **Send credentials** to partner with dashboard access guide

**Owner:** Operations team.
**Maximum time:** 24 hours after approval.

### Stage 5: Welcome

- Send welcome email (see template below)
- Schedule 30-minute onboarding call
- Share approved marketing materials
- Explain compliance rules
- Assign internal point of contact

### Stage 6: First Payout

- The first payment is made per the program calendar (monthly, weekly or daily)
- A manual verification of the first payment is done to ensure amounts are correct
- Confirm with the partner that they received the payment
- From the second payment, the process is automated via Cellxpert

## Monthly Partner Review

### Metrics to Evaluate

| Metric | What It Indicates | Alert Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| FTDs generated in the month | Partner productivity | Less than 1 FTD in 60 days |
| Conversion rate (clicks to FTD) | Traffic quality | Lower than 2% |
| Average deposit of referred clients | Client quality | Lower than $150 |
| Retention of referred clients | Quality and sustainability | Lower than 3 months average |
| Lots per client per month | Trading activity | Lower than 3 lots |
| Chargeback/dispute ratio | Fraud risk | Higher than 5% |
| Marketing materials compliance | Regulatory compliance | Any infraction |

### Tier Upgrade Criteria

Upgrade is reviewed monthly and requires:

1. Meeting the numerical requirements of the higher tier (clients, deposits, FTDs)
2. Maintaining the requirements for at least 2 consecutive months
3. Zero compliance infractions in the last 3 months
4. Team approval (and Principals for Senior+ tiers)

### Tier Downgrade Criteria

Downgrade triggers when:

1. The partner does not meet the minimum requirements of their current tier for 3 consecutive months
2. A serious compliance infraction is detected
3. The chargeback ratio exceeds 10%

**Process:** The partner is notified 30 days in advance. An action plan is offered to recover the tier. If after 30 days there is no improvement, the downgrade is executed.

## Fraud Detection

### Red Flags

| Signal | Risk Level | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Mass FTDs from the same IP | High | Multiple new accounts from one location |
| Immediate minimum deposits without trading | High | Clients depositing exactly the minimum and not trading |
| Self-referral pattern | High | Partner refers themselves or family |
| Bot traffic | High | Mass clicks without conversions, non-human patterns |
| Recurring chargebacks | High | Clients dispute deposits after CPA is paid |
| Clients withdrawing everything in less than 7 days | Medium | Possible churning to generate FTDs |
| Misleading marketing content | Medium | Guaranteed earnings promises, false information |
| FTDs concentrated at end of month | Low-Medium | Possible manipulation to reach tier |

### Investigation Protocol

1. **Automatic detection (Cellxpert):** The platform generates automatic alerts based on predefined patterns.
2. **Initial review (Operations team):** The alert is reviewed within 24 hours.
3. **Escalation (if suspicion is confirmed):** Escalated to Edward for detailed review.
4. **Preventive suspension:** Partner payments are suspended while investigating (maximum 15 business days).
5. **Final decision:**
   - If fraud is confirmed: immediate agreement termination, retention of pending commissions, report to legal team.
   - If false alarm: immediate reactivation with formal apology and payment of withheld commissions.
   - If gray area: formal warning, intensified monitoring for 90 days.

**Who handles:** Edward is the primary point. Serious cases are escalated to Principals.

## Compliance Requirements for Partners

### What Partners CAN Do

- Promote NEOMAAA using approved marketing materials
- Share factual information about spreads, available instruments and platforms
- Offer educational trading content (without earnings promises)
- Use their tracking links in their own channels
- Mention NEOMAAA's regulatory license (Anjouan)
- Share verified real testimonials

### What Partners CANNOT Do

- Promise specific earnings or guaranteed returns
- Make false declarations about NEOMAAA's regulation
- Use NEOMAAA's logo without prior approval
- Send spam or mass unsolicited communications
- Buy traffic from prohibited sources (incentivized traffic, cookie stuffing)
- Create websites that imitate or can be confused with NEOMAAA's official site
- Offer bonuses, cashback or incentives not authorized by NEOMAAA
- Use pressure tactics to make clients deposit
- Handle client funds or act as payment intermediary

### Approved Marketing Materials

All material must be approved before publication:

- Banners and creatives: provided by NEOMAAA, customization with prior approval
- Social media copy: provided templates, modifications with approval
- Videos: script must be reviewed before publication
- Landing pages: must include mandatory regulatory disclaimers

## Escalation Matrix

### Partner Complaints

| Complaint Type | First Line | Escalation | Response Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Question about commissions | Operations team | Edward | 24 hours |
| Tracking discrepancy | Operations team | Edward | 48 hours |
| Tier upgrade request | Edward | Principals (Senior+) | 5 business days |
| Complaint about delayed payments | Finance | Edward | 24 hours |
| Payout dispute (incorrect amount) | Finance | Edward → Principals | 48 hours |
| Special conditions request | Edward | Principals | 5 business days |
| Complaint about another partner (territory) | Edward | Principals | 72 hours |

### Payout Disputes

1. The partner reports the discrepancy
2. Finance reviews the calculation in Cellxpert within 24 hours
3. If the error is NEOMAAA's: immediate correction + payment of the difference in the next cycle
4. If the calculation is correct: detailed breakdown is provided to the partner
5. If the partner does not accept the explanation: escalation to Edward
6. If the dispute persists: review by Principals with final decision in 5 business days

### Compliance Violations

| Severity | Example | Action | Who Decides |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minor | Use of logo without prior approval | Written warning, correction request | Operations team |
| Medium | Content with exaggerated earnings promises | Formal warning, 48 hours to remove content | Edward |
| Serious | Handling client funds, confirmed fraud | Immediate suspension, formal investigation | Principals |
| Critical | Illegal activity, reputational damage | Immediate contract termination | Principals |
