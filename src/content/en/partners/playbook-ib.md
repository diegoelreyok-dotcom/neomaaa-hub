## IB FAQ

### 1. When is my first commission paid?

On the 15th of the month following the first month where you generated volume. Example: your first client makes FTD in April and trades during April → you collect on May 15.

### 2. What happens if a client of mine withdraws everything?

Nothing. The client remains yours for life. If they deposit again at any time, you earn on their volume again.

### 3. Can I bring clients from USA?

No. USA is on the restricted countries list. KYC rejects them automatically and you do not earn commission for those leads.

### 4. Can I offer extra bonuses out of my pocket?

No. Any additional incentive to the client (cashback from you, refund promise, "I give you $50 if you open account") is prohibited. It is informal rebating and compromises broker compliance.

### 5. Can I do copy trading with my clients?

No. You are IB, not money manager. If a client asks you to trade for them, you refuse and explain it is unauthorized discretionary management.

If you want to offer signals legitimately, the correct channel is **MQL5 Signals** (https://www.mql5.com/en/signals), the native MT5 system operated by MetaQuotes. You publish your signal there, your NEOMAAA clients subscribe from their own MT5 to your signal, and you earn the monthly subscription you define (MetaQuotes keeps a share). Each follower maintains their own account and own risk. NEOMAAA **does not offer MAM/PAMM** currently — do not try to trade their account yourself.

### 6. What happens if the client loses and gets angry at me?

You remind them of the risk disclaimer they signed when opening account. You did not guarantee anything (you should not have). The broker does not pass the complaint to you. You continue collecting your past commissions without problem.

### 7. Can I appear in NEOMAAA videos?

Yes, Senior and Elite IBs have access to the NEOMAAA podcast studio and can appear in official content. Coordination through the Partner Manager.

### 8. What happens if I have a conflict with another IB over a client?

The client is attributed to the IB whose **tracking cookie** was registered first at signup (last-click with 90-day window). If there is ambiguity, compliance reviews logs and decides. The decision is final.

### 9. How do I tier up?

Automatic. Each month your volume is recalculated. If you pass the threshold, the tier updates next month. To go down, you need to be 2 consecutive months under the threshold.

### 10. Does my commission go down if my clients stop trading?

Your **tier** may drop if your monthly volume falls under the threshold for 2 consecutive months. Commissions already generated are yours and are not revised.

### 11. Can I use the NEOMAAA logo?

Yes, with the official versions of the logo pack. You cannot modify colors, typography, nor combine with other logos without approval. Silver+ can do limited co-branding.

### 12. Do I need my own constituted company?

Not mandatory for Bronze and Silver. For Senior and Elite it is recommended (or mandatory per jurisdiction) for tax matters. NEOMAAA can pay to individual or legal entity interchangeably.

### 13. Do you pay taxes on my commissions?

NEOMAAA pays without withholding. **You are responsible for declaring and paying tax in your tax residence country.** NEOMAAA can issue monthly payment proof for your accountant.

### 14. Can I do trading signals?

Yes, as long as you respect disclaimers and do not promise profitability. You cannot trade client accounts; only send signals.

### 15. Can I open demo accounts for my clients?

The client opens their own demo through your link. You cannot create accounts in their name. Demo is free and unlimited.

### 16. What if my sub-IB cheats?

Report it immediately to the Partner Manager. If you report them first, it does not splash on you. If compliance detects them before you report, the penalty also reaches you.

### 17. Can I sell the affiliate program to other brokers too?

Yes, there is no exclusivity clause. But if we detect you are doing bait-and-switch (taking NEOMAAA leads to another broker after signup), it is cause for immediate termination.

### 18. What happens if NEOMAAA changes the commission %?

Changes are communicated **60 days in advance** by official email. Clients you already brought keep the % grandfathered for 6 more months after the change.

### 19. Is there an annual partners event?

Yes. Annual in-person event (Dubai in even years, LATAM in odd years) with flight and stay paid for Elite tier. Senior pays own stay but has free entry. Silver and Bronze can attend with paid entry.

### 20. Can I invest as a client too?

Yes. Many IBs also trade. You open a separate trading account (you cannot charge commission to yourself). Self-referral in the same account = automatic fraud.
