## 1. GENERAL COMMUNICATION PRINCIPLE

The entire NEOMAAA team (sales, support, marketing, partners) is PROHIBITED from making performance promises, guaranteeing profits, minimizing risks, or giving investment advice. NEOMAAA is an intermediary, not a financial advisor.

Any employee who commits a violation receives immediate individual training. On the second violation: written warning. On the third: possible termination. See training process in [Compliance Calendar](/content/compliance/compliance-calendar).

## 3. A-BOOK / B-BOOK -- PUBLIC VS CONFIDENTIAL

**Important:** Neomaaa Ltd OFFICIALLY acknowledges in its Terms and Conditions (`legal/terms-conditions.md` and `legal/order-execution-policy.md`) that it may act as principal or counterparty in client trades. Therefore, mentioning that we operate a "hybrid ECN/STP model" or that "Neomaaa may act as principal or counterparty" is APPROVED and public communication.

What is CONFIDENTIAL and for the exclusive use of the Dealing Desk + Principals is the internal routing policy ([ab-book-policy.md](/content/executive/ab-book-policy)): thresholds, ratios, specific criteria, and the A/B classification of individual clients. The sales team does NOT have access to this info and does NOT know whether a specific client is A-Book or B-Book.

| Client question | Approved response (public) | PROHIBITED response |
|---|---|---|
| "Are you A-Book or B-Book?" | "NEOMAAA operates a hybrid ECN/STP model. Orders are routed to liquidity providers based on execution rules. You can audit execution, slippage and times in MT5." | Confirming that a client is A-Book or B-Book. Revealing routing criteria, thresholds, or ratios. |
| "Do you trade against me?" | "Our hybrid ECN/STP model seeks to align interests with the client. Execution is auditable in MT5." | Confirming or denying specific internalization. |
| "I want to make sure you don't close my positions on purpose" | "Orders are processed under our published execution policy (`legal/order-execution-policy.md`). You can review any specific trade with support." | Operational details of the dealing desk. |

If the client insists or asks specific technical questions, escalate to Pepe (Head of Dealing). NEVER invent or confirm anything about the internal model.
