## 1. PROHIBITED PHRASES

None of the phrases in this table may be used in ANY channel (WhatsApp, Telegram, calls, email, chat, social media, printed materials, videos, webinars). Any deliberate variant or paraphrase that produces the same effect is also prohibited.

| # | PROHIBITED PHRASE | WHY IT IS PROBLEMATIC | WHAT TO SAY INSTEAD |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | "I guarantee you'll make money" | Performance promise, illegal in any jurisdiction | "Trading involves risk. Results depend on your strategy and market conditions." |
| 2 | "It's impossible to lose with this strategy" | Performance promise, misleading information | "Every trading strategy carries risk of loss. We recommend practicing first on a demo account." |
| 3 | "Our clients earn X% monthly" | Past performance does not guarantee future, implicit promise | "Results vary between clients. Past performance is not indicative of future results." |
| 4 | "Deposit more and you'll earn more" | Induces depositing more than the client can afford | "We recommend depositing only what you can afford to lose without affecting your personal finances." |
| 5 | "The market is going to go up/down, it's safe" | Investment advice, market prediction | "We do not provide investment advice. We suggest doing your own analysis or consulting an independent advisor." |
| 6 | "I trade this and it goes great for me" | Implicit investment advice, undue influence | "As NEOMAAA employees we are not authorized to give personal trading advice." |
| 7 | "There's no risk" / "It's very safe" / "Minimal risk" | Minimizes the real risk of CFD/forex trading | "CFD and forex trading carries a high risk of loss. You may lose all your invested capital." |
| 8 | "You can withdraw your money whenever you want without issues" | There may be conditions (bonuses, verification, etc.) | "Withdrawals are processed within [X] business days, subject to standard security verification." |
| 9 | "We are the best broker on the market" | Unverifiable claim, misleading advertising | "NEOMAAA offers competitive conditions with spreads from X pips, ECN/STP execution and 2,000+ instruments." |
| 10 | "Your money is 100% safe with us" | Promise that cannot be guaranteed | "Client funds are held in segregated accounts in accordance with applicable regulations." |
| 11 | "I'll manage your account" / "I'll trade for you" | Unauthorized account management, illegal | "NEOMAAA does not offer account management services. Each client is responsible for their own trading decisions." |
| 12 | "Buy/sell [instrument] now" | Direct investment advice | "We are not authorized to give buy or sell recommendations. Trading decisions are the client's responsibility." |
| 13 | "With 1:1000 leverage you can earn a lot with little" | Presents leverage only as an advantage, without mentioning risk | "Leverage amplifies both gains and losses. High leverage may result in losses exceeding your deposit." |
| 14 | "Don't worry about losses, they're easy to recover" | Minimizes losses, induces further trading | "Losses are part of trading. We recommend managing your risk carefully and never trading with money you cannot afford to lose." |
| 15 | "This trade is a unique opportunity" / "Don't miss out" | Artificial urgency, sales pressure | "Every trader should evaluate opportunities based on their own strategy and risk tolerance." |
| 16 | "Forex is better than keeping money in the bank" | Misleading comparison, implies it's a safe investment | "Forex is a high-risk market, different from bank savings products. Not comparable." |
| 17 | "I'll give you a trading signal" | Investment advice, potentially unauthorized management | "NEOMAAA does not provide trading signals. Trading decisions are the exclusive responsibility of the client." |
| 18 | "With us there are no hidden spreads" (if not verifiable) | Claim that must be 100% demonstrable | "Our spreads are transparent and visible on the MT5 platform in real time." |
| 19 | "We'll double your deposit" (referring to bonuses) | Can be interpreted as a profit promise | "We have a X% bonus promotion on your deposit, subject to terms and conditions." |
| 20 | "You don't need experience to earn in forex" | Minimizes complexity, induces trading without preparation | "We recommend that new traders use our demo account to familiarize themselves with the platform before trading with real funds." |
| 21 | "We are regulated by FCA / CySEC / ASIC / BaFin / BCRA / CNV / CNMV" (or any tier-1 or national regulator NEOMAAA does not hold) | FALSE REGULATORY CLAIM -- NEOMAAA's only license is L15968/N from AOFA (Anjouan). Lying about regulation is a crime in almost all jurisdictions | "Neomaaa Ltd, International Business Company registered in Union of Comoros (reg. 15968), International Brokerage License L15968/N granted by the Anjouan Offshore Finance Authority (AOFA). Registered office: Hamchako, Mutsamudu, The Autonomous Island of Anjouan, Union of Comoros." |
| 22 | "Double your money" / "Double your account in X days" / "Triple your capital" | Explicit performance promise with specific figure | "Trading is high risk. Results depend on your strategy, experience and market conditions." |
| 23 | "NEOMAAA is better than [competitor]" / "[Competitor] is a scam, come to NEOMAAA" / Bashing other brokers by name | Direct comparison disparaging a competitor -- illegal in most jurisdictions and generates legal risk | "I invite you to compare conditions point by point live: our Raw account offers spreads from 0.0 pips, $3/lot/side, and 120+ deposit methods." |
| 24 | "Guaranteed profitability" / "Signals that never fail" / "Infallible system" / "100% win rate" | Variants of performance promise | "Past performance does not guarantee future results. Trading carries a risk of loss." |
| 25 | "Unlimited leverage" / "$0 minimum deposit" / "Instant withdrawals with no fee" (when there is a real fee or minimum) | False offer -- real conditions differ from advertised | Report real conditions: "leverage up to 1:1000", "minimum deposit Cent $5 / Standard $50 / Raw $500", "times and fees vary by method" |
| 26 | "Borrow money to invest" / "Use your credit card to deposit more" | Inducement to debt to trade | "Only trade with capital you can afford to lose without affecting your personal finances." |
| 27 | "Don't worry about KYC, it's just paperwork" / "I can speed up your verification" | Minimizes compliance and/or creates false expectation | "KYC is a mandatory regulatory requirement. The standard process takes 24-48h and protects your funds." |
| 28 | "I'll give you investment advice" / "Buy/sell [X] now" / "I'll tell you when to enter and exit" | Unlicensed investment advisory | "NEOMAAA does not provide investment recommendations. Trading decisions are the exclusive responsibility of the client." |

## 3. MANDATORY DISCLAIMERS

The following disclaimers MUST appear in all client communication. They are the OFFICIAL texts approved by Neomaaa Ltd.

### 3.1 Short disclaimer (for messages, WhatsApp, social media)

> "CFD and forex trading carries a high risk of loss. 70-80% of retail clients lose money trading CFDs. You may lose all your capital. Do not invest money you cannot afford to lose."

### 3.2 Long disclaimer (for emails, landing pages, promotional material)

> "Risk warning: Contracts for difference (CFDs) and forex trading are leveraged products that carry a high level of risk to your capital. 70-80% of retail clients lose money trading CFDs with this type of provider. You should not invest money that you cannot afford to lose. Before deciding to trade, you should carefully consider your investment objectives, level of experience and risk tolerance. There is a possibility that you may lose part or all of your initial investment. You must be aware of all risks associated with trading CFDs and forex, including slippage, latency, requotes and execution delays. Neomaaa Ltd operates on an execution-only basis: it does not offer investment advice or portfolio management. If in doubt, seek independent financial advice. Past performance is not indicative of future results. Neomaaa Ltd (IBC 15968), International Brokerage License L15968/N granted by the Anjouan Offshore Finance Authority (AOFA). Registered office: Hamchako, Mutsamudu, The Autonomous Island of Anjouan, Union of Comoros."

### 3.3 Execution-only disclaimer (when client asks for opinions or signals)

> "Neomaaa Ltd operates on an execution-only basis. We do not provide investment advice, personalized recommendations, trading signals or portfolio management. Any trading decision is the exclusive responsibility of the client."

### 3.4 Counterparty disclaimer (when client asks about execution model)

> "Neomaaa Ltd may act as principal or counterparty in its clients' trades, within the framework of its published order execution policy. Execution is auditable in MetaTrader 5."

### 3.5 Leverage disclaimer (when leverage is mentioned)

> "Leverage amplifies both potential gains and potential losses. Leverage of 1:1000 means that a 0.1% move against your position may result in the total loss of your margin. Make sure you fully understand how leverage works before trading."
