## Risk Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Average FTD Drops 30% (from $300 to $210)

| Impact | Adjusted Value |
|---|---|
| Lifetime deposits | $1,260 (vs $1,800) |
| Estimated lots/month | 7 (vs 10) |
| Gross monthly revenue | $70 - $88 (vs $100 - $125) |
| Adjusted LTV | $700 - $980 (vs $1,000 - $1,400) |
| Mini-CPA Brazil payback | 1.5 months (vs 1 month) |
| Hybrid margin (Starter) | 55% (vs 62%) |

**Required action:** If average FTD drops below $210, review Mini-CPAs by GEO and consider reducing $20-$30 per FTD. Keep LTV/CPA ratio above 6x.

### Scenario 2: Retention Drops to 3 Months (from 6 months)

| Impact | Adjusted Value |
|---|---|
| Lifetime deposits | $900 (vs $1,800) |
| Lifetime lots | 30 (vs 60) |
| Adjusted LTV | $500 - $700 (vs $1,000 - $1,400) |
| Mini-CPA Brazil payback | 1 month (no change in month 1) |
| Affiliate Partner margin (Partner tier) | 58% (vs 66%) |
| IB Bronze margin (Rev Share, 30%) | 55% (vs 61%) |

**Required action:** If retention drops below 4 months, activate aggressive retention program (deposit bonuses, trading signals, exclusive events). Review Creator Rewards Lab bounty structure.

### Scenario 3: Combined (FTD -30% + 3-month Retention)

| Impact | Adjusted Value |
|---|---|
| Adjusted LTV | $350 - $490 |
| LTV/CPA ratio (Brazil hybrid) | 3.5x - 4.9x |
| Starter hybrid margin | 42% |

**Required action:** This scenario threatens the viability of partnership programs. Immediate actions:
1. Freeze onboarding of new partners
2. Reduce Mini-CPA across all GEOs by 40%
3. Temporarily suspend Content Bounties and Equipment Milestones
4. Renegotiate high tiers with existing partners

## Margin Safety Thresholds

### When to Stop Onboarding Partners

| Warning Signal | Threshold | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Net margin per client drops below | 40% | Pause onboarding of new partners in high tiers |
| LTV/CPA ratio drops below | 5x | Review Mini-CPAs and consider reduction |
| Monthly cash burn from CPAs exceeds | 30% of gross monthly revenue | Freeze new Digital Affiliates |
| Average client retention drops below | 4 months | Activate retention plan before adding partners |
| % of clients from a single partner exceeds | 25% of total | Diversify; do not approve more clients from that partner |

### Exposure Limits per Individual Partner

| Partner Type | Client Limit | Dependent Revenue Limit |
|---|---|---|
| Affiliate Partner | No hard limit | Maximum 15% of total revenue |
| Digital Affiliate | No hard limit | Maximum 20% of total revenue |
| Individual IB | 500 clients max | Maximum 25% of total revenue |

If an individual partner exceeds these limits, clients are not removed, but acceptance of new clients from that partner is frozen until the rest of the portfolio grows.

## Financial Recommendations for Principals

1. **Start with Affiliate Partners** -- zero fixed cost, minimum risk, allows product validation.
2. **Activate Digital Affiliates in month 2-3** -- only when there's enough recurring revenue to cover CPAs.
3. **IB Program in month 4+** -- requires stabilized operations to handle Monthly Milestones.
4. **Maintain cash reserve** equivalent to 3 months of projected CPAs.
5. **Review margins weekly** for the first 12 weeks. If margin drops below 50%, take immediate corrective action.
6. **The hybrid model is the default offer.** Only offer pure Rev Share to partners with proven track record.
7. **The 12-Month Super-Bonus of $50K + equity** only activates for IBs that generate a minimum of $500K in accumulated deposits.
